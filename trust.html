This page explains how our CRS recommendations are generated, what data is used, and how you control your account data.

Methodology transparency

  • CRS outputs are calculated from your submitted profile fields and rule-based scoring logic.
  • Recommendation panels prioritize opportunities by estimated gain, effort, and timeline signals.
  • Freshness labels indicate how recent draw/category snapshots are.

Guidance is informational and strategic in nature; it is not legal advice.

Data handling

  • Profile and progress data can be stored locally on your device for continuity.
  • If authenticated, selected profile data can be synced through configured backend services.
  • Analytics events are limited to product-improvement signals and avoid sensitive free-text content.

User controls

  • Update display and contact preferences via the Account panel.
  • Reset local progress from calculator restart flows.
  • Delete account using the account danger-zone action (when backend deletion is configured).

Policy and source reliability

  • Source and freshness indicators are shown in results sections.
  • If live integrations are unavailable, fallback datasets are used and labeled.
  • Always verify final eligibility and policy details on official IRCC sources.

Need help?

For privacy or data-rights requests, use the project support contact listed in your product footer/legal pages.
