This page explains how our CRS recommendations are generated, what data is used, and how you control your account data.

Methodology transparency

  • CRS outputs are calculated from your submitted profile fields and rule-based scoring logic.
  • Recommendation panels prioritize opportunities by estimated gain, effort, timeline, confidence, and profile-lane fit signals.
  • Freshness labels indicate how recent draw/category snapshots are.
  • High-risk warnings are shown when your score gap or profile uncertainty materially increases decision risk.

Guidance is informational and strategic in nature; it is not legal advice.

Data handling

  • Profile and progress data can be stored locally on your device for continuity.
  • If authenticated, selected profile data can be synced through configured backend services.
  • Analytics events are limited to product-improvement signals and avoid sensitive free-text content.
  • Analytics payloads include schema/version and session metadata for quality monitoring, not identity profiling.

User controls

  • Update display and contact preferences via the Account panel.
  • Reset local progress from calculator restart flows.
  • Delete account using the account danger-zone action (when backend deletion is configured).
  • Use unsubscribe links to disable draw alert notifications at any time.

Policy and source reliability

  • Source and freshness indicators are shown in results sections.
  • If live integrations are unavailable, fallback datasets are used and labeled.
  • Always verify final eligibility and policy details on official IRCC sources.

Model limitations and decision safety

  • Score projections are directional estimates and can be impacted by policy updates, intake caps, and document timing.
  • Execution plans assume consistent weekly follow-through; missed milestones can reduce projected gains.
  • For regulated advice, legal interpretation, or case representation, consult qualified immigration professionals.

Retention and deletion expectations

  • Local browser data remains until you clear it, reset the app, or delete storage manually.
  • Cloud-synced records depend on your backend deployment configuration and retention policies.
  • When account deletion is enabled and completed, related user account access is removed.
